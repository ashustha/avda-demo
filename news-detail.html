Corporate Advantage Acquires Blueprint Accounting

Corporate Advantage is excited to announce a major milestone in our growth and expansion: the acquisition of Blueprint Accounting.

Corporate Advantage is excited to announce a major milestone in our growth and expansion: the acquisition of Blueprint Accounting.

Blueprint Accounting is a well-established accounting firm known for its high-quality service and commitment to its clients. We are thrilled to welcome the talented team at Blueprint Accounting into the Corporate Advantage family. This acquisition allows us to expand our range of services and offer an even wider range of specialized solutions to our clients.

With the merger, our clients will have access to a stronger team of professionals, including forensic accounting and business advisory services. We believe that this merger will bring significant value to our clients and have a positive impact on their business growth. We understand that every client has specific needs and requirements for their business. With this merger, we can better serve those needs and provide the highest level of service and support to you.

The principal of Blueprint Accounting Services, Michael Zhu, will join Corporate Advantage as the Partner – Business Services, Australia. The entire Blueprint Accounting team will also be joining Corporate Advantage, ensuring a seamless transition for clients.

We are confident that this merger will bring significant value to our clients and have a positive impact on their business growth. We are excited to embark on this new chapter and to continue serving our clients to the best of our abilities. If you have any questions or would like to learn more about the additional services available to you, please do not hesitate to reach out to us.

Thank you for your continued support and trust in Corporate Advantage. We look forward to working with you and helping you achieve your business goals.
